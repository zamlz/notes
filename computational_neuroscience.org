Computational Neuroscience is a subfield of [[file:neuroscience.org][Neuroscience]] that focuses on modelling neuroscience using [[file:computer_science.org][Computer Science]]. There is also some focus on [[file:artificial_intelligence.org][Artificial Intelligence]] as the research areas can also focus on simulating behaviour of the brain.
