Computational Neuroscience is a subfield of [[id:8d203170-2b5a-47de-83c8-d5e9e08cbf9a][Neuroscience]] that focuses on modelling neuroscience using [[id:d60549a6-eb6e-4e98-a944-1909e4a73a25][Computer Science]]. There is also some focus on [[id:ef099bbc-4947-416f-8426-45568b9773c3][Artificial Intelligence]] as the research areas can also focus on simulating behaviour of the brain.
